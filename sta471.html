STA 471 - Regression Analysis

Overview

This course covers a variety of regression techniques such as simple linear, multiple linear, curvilinear, and multiple curvilinear modeling. Variable selection techniques and model adequacy will also be discussed. A famous statistician (R. A. Fisher) once said, "Statistics can be regarded as the study of populations, as the study of variations, and as the study of methods of the reduction of data." We will come to understand how this statement made in 1925 still applies today and how regression analysis can be used to make quality decisions.

The lectures are very important for understanding the material, completing the homework, and doing well on the exams. Please take notes during class and ask questions if you need a topic clarified. I strongly encourage you to make use of my office hours. This is a great way to get extra help on lecture material, homework questions, and exam preparation.
