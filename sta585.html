STA 585 - Statistical Consulting Practicum

Overview

This course provides statistical consulting experience in a supervised lab setting. It involves interacting with clients, recommending analyses, and reviewing results. A famous statistician (R. A. Fisher) once said, "Statistics can be regarded as the study of populations, as the study of variations, and as the study of methods of the reduction of data." We will come to understand how this statement made in 1925 still applies today and how statistical consulting serves as a practical way to make decisions based on data.
